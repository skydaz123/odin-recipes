Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly
