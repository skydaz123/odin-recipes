Beef Wellington

Description

This is the hardest recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds beef tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • ½ cup sliced fresh mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Place beef in a small baking dish
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat. Saute onion and mushrooms in butter for 5 minutes. Remove from heat, and let cool.
  3. Mix together pate and 2 tablespoons softened butter, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Roll out the puff pastry dough, and place beef in the center. Fold up, and seal all the edges
  5. Bake at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) for 10 minutes
  6. Place all reserved juices in a small saucepan over high heat.
